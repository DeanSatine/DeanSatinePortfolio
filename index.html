About Me

Hey! I'm a systems and character designer who's incredibly passionate about creation with constraints. I love to make cool designs with as small as a scope as possible. I love socializing and working with others, and there's so much for me to learn!

I’m incredibly passionate about uplifting and encouraging others, and my performance in the Game Development Student Association (GDSA) has been reflective of that for the last 3 years!

I am currently attending Ontario Tech University's Game Development & Interactive Program as a part of the Game Development Student Association! I hope you're liking what you see!
