About Me

I’m a systems and character designer passionate about creating engaging and meaningful systematic designs.

My approach combines analytical thinking with creative vision, ensuring that every character feels distinct to play and every system serves both gameplay and narrative purposes. Whether working on combat systems, progression frameworks, or character rosters, I strive to craft designs that are elegant, intuitive, and full of depth.
