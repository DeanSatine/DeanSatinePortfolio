About the Project

Made for the annual GDSA Fall GD Game Jam 2025!

Welcome to your new 9-to-9 shift at Subway, where the sandwiches are unlimited, the customers are hostile, and rent is somehow both your only motivation and your greatest enemy. Meet Dean: armed with nothing but his inexplicable ability to summon footlongs out of thin air and a narrator who won't stop psychoanalyzing his life choices.

My Responsibilities

  • As a mentor, taught a 1st year game designer to create a low-scoped game loop
  • Programmed & implemented all elements within Unity
