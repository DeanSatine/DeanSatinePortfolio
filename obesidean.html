About the Project

Made for the annual GDSA Fall GD Game Jam 2025!
This is the story of a man named Dean.

Welcome to your new 9-to-9 shift at Subway, where the sandwiches are unlimited, the customers are hostile, and rent is somehow both your only motivation and your greatest enemy.

Meet Dean: a completely ordinary sandwich artist trapped in an extraordinarily strange predicament. Armed with nothing but his inexplicable ability to summon footlongs out of thin air and a narrator who won't stop psychoanalyzing his life choices, Dean must defend his counter from increasingly aggressive flash mobs who really, really want sandwiches.
