About the Project

Play as a blue-collared wagie in a world full of cats & dogs — except you're getting fired. Literally. To avoid being incinerated, you must sneak around the office, using anything at your disposal to fight back and stay hidden. Find & destroy your boss's prized mugs around the office to track him down and seek your revenge!

Created as a 2nd Year Game for Gamecon at Ontario Tech University.

My Responsibilities

  • Led a team of 6 people
  • Designed 10 unique weapons
  • Designed progression & mug system
  • Created various prop 3D models
