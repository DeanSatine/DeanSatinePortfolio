Trisoul Tower

About the Project

Into the Chamber! Play as a wandering warrior, as you learn to master the different swords and their elements, Ice, Fire, and Nature! In this hack & slash action adventure, take down the boss, and their minions as you master the different weapons and unleash the magic upon the land!

Created as a 3rd year game for GameCon at Ontario Tech University.

My Responsibilities

  • Redesigned and pivoted the game's direction to lower overall scope
  • Designed and implemented the player combat system and environmental physics interactions
  • Created 3 unique enemy archetypes (2 basic scopable enemies and a final boss) with distinct behaviours
  • Designed 3 elemental & gameplay variety swords with unique ability mechanics
  • Created all VFX for the game
