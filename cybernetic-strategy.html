About the Project

A prototype of an auto battler made to demonstrate my understandings of systems design as well as create a framework for 1-5 cost units, unit Origins & Classes and augment designs.

This prototype was created to demonstrate my understanding and ability to innovate in this space and create frameworks for units, traits & systems in order to expand on the project for an entire set in the future. This project displays my ability due to me creating it within a month.
