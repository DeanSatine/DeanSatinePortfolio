About the Project

An auto battler prototype built to demonstrate my knowledge of Teamfight Tactics. Features a unique space and robot theme, a unit of each cost tier, 2 unique Origins, 2 Classes, and 3 Augments!

The design of the systems and units was meant to display my ability to create characters and traits that are as low-scoped as possible while still being interesting — proven by completing the entire project within 3 weeks.
